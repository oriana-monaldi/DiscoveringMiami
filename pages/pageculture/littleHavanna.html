Little Havanna

A small part of Cuba remains alive within the United States in one of the most well-known neighborhoods in all of Miami, Little Havana.

Life in Little Havana revolves around Calle Ocho. One of the most charming places in Little Havana is located on Calle Ocho at the intersection of 15th Avenue; it's the Maximo Gomez Park, a place where elderly Cubans gather to play dominoes or chess while trying to solve the world's problems by discussing the past and future of Cuba.
