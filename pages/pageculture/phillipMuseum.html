The Phillip & Patricia Frost Museum of Science

The Phillip & Patricia Frost Museum of Science, located in Miami, Florida, is a fascinating destination for science enthusiasts of all ages.

With interactive and educational exhibitions, the museum offers an immersive experience covering topics such as biodiversity, physics, astronomy, and technology.
