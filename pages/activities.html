Activities

Ocean Drive

Is the first place you have to go once you arrive in Miami. On one side, you'll have the most visited beaches in the entire city, and on the other, an abundance of top-notch shops. Here, you can go shopping, have a drink by the sea, or even find accommodation.

Celebrities Home Boat Tour

Enjoy breathtaking views of Miami from the water with our boat tours of Biscayne Bay. Discover the city's famous skyscrapers, celebrity mansions, and the natural beauty of the bay as you relax aboard a comfortable boat.

Everglades Excurison

On this excursion, we'll explore the Everglades, the wildest area in the United States. We'll navigate the wetlands on an airboat and you'll have the chance to see native alligators up close

Night Party Cruise in Miami

A floating party on the lower deck, while the upper deck offers unparalleled views of Miami's magnificent nighttime skyline. The best wayto see Miami after dark is from the water
