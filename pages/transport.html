Miami Metrobus

The Metrorail is an elevated train that runs from north to south in Miami, with two lines covering 40 kilometers and serving 23 stations.

Some of the stops on the Miami Metrorail are located at tourist attractions such as Coconut Grove, the Vizcaya Museum, Brickell Avenue, and Government Center.
