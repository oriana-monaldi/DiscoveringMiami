Beach

Sunny Island Beach

Situated between Miami Beach and Fort Lauderdale, Sunny Isles Beach is known for its stunning oceanfront skyscrapers and cosmopolitan atmosphere. This white sandy beach offers a wide range of amenities, including oceanfront parks, boardwalks, and a variety of water activities. With its blend of luxury and accessibility, Sunny Isles Beach attracts visitors from all over in search of sun, sea, and fun.

South Beach

World-renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and iconic stretch of white sand, South Beach is one of Miami's most famous beaches. Located in the heart of Miami Beach, this beach offers a unique combination of art deco architecture, lively nightlife, crystal-clear waters, and a wide variety of activities. From sunbathing to strolling along the famous Ocean Drive, South Beach is a must-visit destination for Miami visitors.

Virginia Key Beach

Vizcaya Beach, also known as Virginia Key Beach, is a hidden gem in Miami. Located on Virginia Key Island, this beach offers a quieter and more relaxed atmosphere compared to other busier beaches in the area. With its lush natural surroundings, calm waters, and panoramic views of the Miami skyline, Vizcaya Beach is the perfect place to escape the city's hustle and bustle and enjoy the serenity of the natural environment.

Palm Beach

Palm Beach, Miami, is a luxurious tropical destination renowned for its white sandy beaches, vibrant dining scene, and elegant architecture. It offers water activities, upscale shopping on Worth Avenue, and a variety of cultural and sports options. It's a must-visit for those seeking an exclusive vacation experience on the southeast coast of the United States.
