Night life

Nikki Beach

One of the most famous and exclusive clubs in Miami. Sunday nights stand out, where the nightclub takes place from 11pm to 5am.

Liv Nightclub

It's a large and very popular nightclub. The musical style can vary depending on the day of the week, but overall it's house, hip-hop, and rock. The most famous DJs spin on its dance floors, such as DJ Tiesto, David Guetta.

Mango Tropical Cafe

At Mango Tropical Café, you can dance salsa, merengue, reggaeton, bachata, and samba until the early hours Sometimes there's a live band and dance shows.

Sroty Nightclub

One of the most famous and popular nightclubs in Miami. A great place to party and dance to the rhythm of the best DJs of the moment. It's important to reserve tickets well in advance as they sell out quickly.

Do Not Six on the Furniture

Do you want to enter the nightclub quickly, without long lines, and access VIP rooms? You should check out Do Not Sit on the Furniture in the center of Miami Beach. It's a much more affordable nightclub where the focus is on good music and great dancing.

Trade

A very small venue but meticulously decorated. It's a highly alternative and unique nightclub, boasting its own sound system. Here you'll find underground DJs spinning deep house, techno, minimalist, and more.
