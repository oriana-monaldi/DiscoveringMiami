Bayside Marketplace

Hours:

Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM

Sundays from 10:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Bayside Marketplace is a shopping center with over 150 stores, restaurants, and terraces overlooking the marina. This pleasant spot for strolling, dining, and enjoying concerts, fireworks, and laser shows is located in Downtown Miami.
