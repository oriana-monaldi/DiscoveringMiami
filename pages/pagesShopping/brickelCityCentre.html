Brickel City Centre

Hours:

Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 09:00 PM

Sundays from 10:00 PM to 07:00 PM

Brickell City Centre stands out for its contemporary design and its focus on integrated urban living. It offers a unique combination of luxury shops, top-notch restaurants, and entertainment all in one place. Additionally, its innovative architecture and focus on sustainability make it stand out as a modern and environmentally-conscious destination in the heart of Miami.
